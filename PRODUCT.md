# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Startup founders, tech recruiters, and engineering teams looking for high-quality frontend craftsmanship, creative developers, and internship/collaboration opportunities.

## Product Purpose
A premium, design-driven single-page portfolio website that showcases software engineering talent, visual motion craft, and digital product creation with extreme detail.

## Brand Personality
Bold, Modern, Creative, and Professional. Editorial restraint combined with striking high-contrast typography, premium 3D assets, and fluid scrolltelling motion.

## Anti-references
- "Cringe" or overused portfolio tropes (e.g. generic illustration icons, SaaS grids).
- Over-rounded containers and soft, wide box shadows on every card ("ghost-card" styling).
- Unnecessary visual noise or decoration.

## Design Principles
1. **Typography as Structure**: Large, ultra-bold Archivo typographic layouts carry the grid, removing the need for generic icons or placeholder graphics.
2. **Tactile & Material Craft**: Leveraging physical design cues (like organic paper grain overlays, warm paper backgrounds, and premium 3D assets) to make the screen feel tactile and expensive.
3. **Liquid Scrolltelling**: Every scroll event coordinates naturally with the interface, dynamically transitioning colors, rotating cards, and revealing layout elements smoothly.
4. **Cinematic Spacing**: Large vertical sections that isolate ideas, ensuring only one cohesive message dominates the viewport at a time.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast body text (WCAG AA compliant) against the `#FAF7F3` background.
- Support for `prefers-reduced-motion` to offer instant layouts for users who prefer static presentation.
